Dave Stanke is a Google Cloud customer engineer and contributor to the [DORA](https://dora.dev) program. He specializes in DevOps, Site Reliability Engineering (SRE), and other flavors of technical relationship therapy. He loves chatting with practitioners: listening to stories, telling stories, sharing a healthy cry. Prior to Google, he was the CTO of OvationTix, a SaaS startup in the performing arts industry, where he specialized in feeding memory to Java servers. He chose on purpose to live in New Jersey, where he enjoys carpentry, indie rock, and fatherhood.

> How to pronounce my last name: **"STANK-eee"** (yes, really)
